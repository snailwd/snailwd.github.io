**Product Manager / Shanghai Pinlan Data Technology Co., Ltd.**

At **Pinlan**, a company focused on the independent R&D and industrial application of the **"AI + CAD"** core platform, I was responsible for managing the development of the **AI-powered construction drawing automation product module**. The company leverages AI, BIM, big data, and cloud-native technologies to enhance the design and construction process, empowering the digital transformation of key sectors such as **Architecture, Engineering, and Construction (AEC)** and **Manufacturing (MFG)**. 

Below are the key contributions I made during my time at Pinlan:

- **Owned the AI-powered construction drawing automation product module**  
  Defined the **MVP (Minimum Viable Product)** and iterated it through agile processes to complete the **PMF (Product-Market Fit)**.

- **Measured business value and defined product modules**  
  Conducted detailed analysis on product value and target customers, identifying critical product challenges. Defined clear acceptance criteria and scope for success.

- **Designed MVP and planned roadmap for AI-generated construction drawings**  
  Focused on delivering a **minimum viable module** instead of all-inclusive drawings for various disciplines. Worked with industry experts, customers, and R&D teams to define market opportunities and technical boundaries. Prioritized the product roadmap, delivering **20+ user stories** in areas like intelligent recognition, rule-based design, and platform interaction.

- **Managed product lifecycle**  
  Led product development and delivery by creating comprehensive product documentation and prototypes. Organized bi-weekly product iterations through daily stand-ups, sprint reviews, and retrospectives. Actively managed the sprint goals, product backlog, and feature launches to ensure alignment with stakeholders.

- **Followed up on product release**  
  Monitored **NPS indicators** and improved the modular drawing process based on user feedback. Enhanced UI/UX design for critical features, like adding pre-drawing verification to improve user experience and drive positive NPS results.

- **Supported go-to-market strategy**  
  Promoted the product to core users, which resulted in **12 primary accounts** with front-line design institutes and **100 designer sub-accounts** during the public beta phase.

- **Optimized internal cloud document product**  
  Prioritized feedback from architects, engineers, and MEP designers against limited development resources. Delivered iterative product versions and created user manuals and training sessions to ease onboarding.

---

This experience at Pinlan allowed me to leverage my skills in **AI-driven product management**, **cross-functional leadership**, and **market strategy** to contribute to the digital transformation in the AEC and MFG industries. My work helped streamline the intelligent design process, improving **collaboration, efficiency, and standardization** across engineering design.
