*- Owned AI-powered construction drawing automation product module and defined MVP (Minimum Viable Product) to complete PMF (Product-Market Fit) by agile iteration*


- *Measured business value and defined product module.* Analyzed product value and target customers. Identified the key and difficult points of product modules and clarified the acceptance criteria and scope of success

- *Designed MVP and planned roadmap of AI generated construction drawings.* Abstracted minimum available module rather than drawings for all majors. Clarified market opportunities and technical boundaries by working closely with industry experts, typical customers and key R&D personnel. Prioritized product roadmap and delivered 20+ user stories in drawing intelligent recognition, rule-based automatic design and platform interaction module

- *Managed product lifecycle. Led implementation of product development and delivery.* Accomplished product document and prototype. Iterated product bi-weekly by organizing daily stand-up, sprint review and retrospective. Took leadership in scrum team to focus on sprint goal and develop product backlog with priorities. Coordinated launches of new features with stakeholders internally and externally for product vision and outcomes alignment

- *Followed up product release.* Oversaw NPS indicators, streamlined the modular drawing process based on user feedback and clarified information design of the pages such as input/output and drawing review. Optimized critical features affecting success rate of AI drawings, such as adding to-do verification before drawing to improve users' ease of use and achieve a positive NPS indicator

- *Supported go-to-market strategy.* Evangelized product for core users which created 12 primary accounts of front-line design institutes and 100 designer sub-accounts in public beta stage

- *Optimized internal cloud document product.* Traded off feedback from architects, structural engineers, and MEP designers against limited development resources for internal product and delivered iterative product versions. Documented user manuals and organized training sessions to reduce the learning curve for newcomers
